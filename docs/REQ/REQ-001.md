## 1. Goal

A single-player poker game.
Use one standard 52-card deck.
Draw 5 cards per round.
The player may exchange cards once per round and may replace only one card.
Play a total of 3 rounds.


## 2. Rules

### Score by Hand Rank
- Straight Flush
- Four of a Kind
- Full House
- Flush
- Straight
- Three of a Kind
- Two Pairs

### Hand Rank Score Table

- Straight Flush: 7 points
- Four of a Kind: 6 points
- Full House: 5 points
- Flush: 4 points
- Straight: 3 points
- Three of a Kind: 2 points
- Two Pairs: 1 point
- Any unlisted hand rank: 0 points

### Pass Condition

Pass if the total score across 3 rounds is 7 points or higher.

## 3. Acceptance

- [ ] AC 1: Success when the 3-round total is 7 points or higher.
- [ ] AC 2: Failure when the 3-round total is 0 to 6 points.
- [ ] AC 3: Each round is scored according to the hand rank score table, and any unlisted hand rank scores 0.
